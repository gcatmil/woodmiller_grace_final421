Welcome to my creatives news space. My name is Philippe; I am an 11 year old from France. Many people know me for my advancements in the ocology field, but I am also an avid inventor and lover of the STEM field. I hope to show off my designs and progress here to inspire creative problem solving around the world. through this process, I hope that my own abilities are able to show improvement overtime, and launch me into a successful life.

The different pages on my website can be found as follows:

    RESEARCH
    INVENTIONS
    PERSONAL
    RUBRIKS
    CLIENTS

These pages help demonstrate my research, my inventions, some of my personal life and my rubriks cube activities. As I am young, I am hoping to grow over time, and contribute to each field (medical or engineering) more into the future. Whenever I do, there will be avid updates and disclosure as it occurs. I don't have any current products out, but I hope to offer open-source code and programming tips in the future so that others may be able to make their own Rubriks Robot. If you have any other inquiries regarding me, my background, or what I do, please head over to the contact page.
