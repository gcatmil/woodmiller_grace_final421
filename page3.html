About

My personal life is nothing interesting. Rather, it is what you'd expect of an 11-year old boy. I attend school, I am in sixth grade, I have no siblings, but I do have external family such as my previously mentioned cousin Chloe. I don't do any sports, but I play piano. We have a dog at home, a retired truffle-hunting black English Labrador named Shelly.

A habit I wish to kick by the end of this year is smoking. I smoke at least a pack of cigarettes a day, if not more. I realize the detrimental effects it has upon any body, especially mine at such an age. But it is hard being a child inventor and navigating the oncology world even with parental help.
